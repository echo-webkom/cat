# The Art of Napping

Cats sleep between 12 and 16 hours per day. This is not a bug, it is a feature refined over thousands of years of evolution.

## Optimal Napping Spots

1. The sunny patch on the living room floor, precisely 40 cm wide
2. On top of the laptop keyboard, exactly when someone needs it
3. The laundry basket, preferably full of dark clothes

## The Science

REM sleep in cats is shorter but more frequent than in humans. This means cats cycle through dreams rapidly — likely explaining the sudden midnight sprints down the hallway.
