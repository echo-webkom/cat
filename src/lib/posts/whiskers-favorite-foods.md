# Whiskers' Favorite Foods

After years of careful observation and scientific rigor (read: letting my cat reject bowl after bowl), I have compiled the definitive ranking of cat foods that actually pass the Whiskers test.

## The Top Picks

**Tuna in spring water** remains the undisputed champion. The moment the can opener clicks, Whiskers materializes from whatever dimension he inhabits.

**Chicken pâté** comes in a close second. Smooth texture, neutral smell for humans, apparently divine for cats.

## What to Avoid

Anything with vegetables mixed in. Cats are obligate carnivores and they know it, even if the marketing copy does not.
