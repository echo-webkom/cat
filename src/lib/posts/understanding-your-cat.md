# Understanding Your Cat

Cats communicate constantly. The problem is that humans have spent millennia misreading the signals.

## Tail Language

A **raised tail** is a greeting — the feline equivalent of a wave. A **puffed tail** means genuine alarm. A **low, slow swish** is focused attention, not necessarily aggression.

## The Slow Blink

If your cat makes eye contact and slowly closes their eyes, blink back slowly. This is trust. Do not underestimate it.

## Chirping

The small chattering sound cats make watching birds through the window is called chirping. It is believed to be a frustrated hunting vocalization, or possibly just cats being weird. Both explanations are valid.
